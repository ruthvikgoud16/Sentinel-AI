## Scoring Guide
- **Alignment to Theme:** Does it clearly solve the hackathon's prompt without a convoluted explanation?
- **Judge "Wow" Factor:** Is the demo visually impressive and conceptually innovative?
- **Feasibility:** Can we actually build this with our current stack in 48 hours without high risk?
- **UI Potential:** Does this idea lend itself to a beautiful, highly interactive frontend?
- **AI Integration Value:** Is Gemini used natively in a way that provides real value, or is it just tacked on?
